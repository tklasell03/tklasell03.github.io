Spring Break 2025

Lisbon → Barcelona → Prague

This spring break, I explored three incredible cities across Europe—Lisbon, Barcelona, and Prague. Each place had its own unique charm, culture, and vibe.

Lisbon
Colorful streets and trams, and the best pastel de nata I've ever had.
Barcelona
Stunning architecture by Gaudi, beaches, and nightlife.
Prague
Gothic buildings and cheap food and drinks it looks out of a movie.
